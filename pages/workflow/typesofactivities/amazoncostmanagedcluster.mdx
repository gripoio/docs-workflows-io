#### <b>Overview:</b>
The Amazon Cost Management activity lets you track and manage your AWS spending directly in GRiPO. It helps monitor cloud usage, send cost alerts, and automate actions to reduce unnecessary expenses. This keeps your cloud operations efficient and budget-friendly.

<b>Examples:</b>

* Get alerts when AWS costs exceed a set limit.
* Automatically generate daily or monthly cost reports.
* Shut down unused services to save money.
