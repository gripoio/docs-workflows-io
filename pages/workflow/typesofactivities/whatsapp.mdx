#### <b>Overveiw</b>
The WhatsApp activity connects GRiPO with WhatsApp to send real-time messages. It allows you to receive alerts, workflow results, and updates directly on your phone, helping you stay informed even when away from your dashboard.

<b>Examples:</b>

* Send instant alerts when an issue occurs.
* Get success or status updates on WhatsApp.
* Trigger workflows using WhatsApp messages.
